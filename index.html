Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026

Browser Extension Cleaner does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

All analysis is performed locally within the user's browser. The extension uses Chrome's extension management API only to display information about installed extensions, including extension names, enabled or disabled status, permissions, and related metadata. This information is used solely to help users review and manage their own installed extensions.

Browser Extension Cleaner does not use analytics, tracking scripts, remote code, advertising SDKs, or third-party data processors.

No extension data is sent to external servers.
